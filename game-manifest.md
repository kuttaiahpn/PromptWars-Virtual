# Prince of Earth:Space Odyssey
   - **Game Story:**
   This game is a web browser and mobile friendly game which is a modern futuristic take on the DOS version of the retro game - Prince of Persia. Not be a clone but still holds spirit of the old version.
   Similar to the storyline of the original game, here our beautiful Princess has been taken hostage but this time by an alien lord of the dark world named Ravun living in a planet named S4T4N far away in the outer space. This planet has similar living conditions of planet Earth and this discovery has drawn their attention to get full control of Earth and rule it as their own. Our Prince is determined to go all out and bring back the Princess home and also finish the entire alien race to save Earth from such future misadventures.

## Global Mechanics
- **Timer:** 3600 seconds (1 hour). Real-time.
- **Health:** 100 HP. Depletes on spikes/enemy hits.
- **Portals:** Level entry points that trigger a "Mission Briefing" UI.

## Level Hierarchy
1. **Level 1 (The Outskirts):**
   - **Goal:** Collect 3 Magic Potions + Upgrade Ship. Eliminate Level 1 war zone commander - Shakun.
   - **Boss:** Shakun (Alien Commander).
   - **Mechanic:** Basic platforming + Shooting.
2. **Level 2 (The War Room):**
   - **Goal:** Recruit 4 AI Drones (Army) + Resource Management. Eliminate Level 2 war zone commander - Katan.
   - **Boss:** Katan (Tactical Commander).
   - **Mechanic:** Strategy-lite (commanding units while fighting).
3. **Level 3 (The Core of S4T4N):**
   - **Goal:** Epic dramatic battle scenes. Reach Ravun's Throne and eliminate. Take Princess and return back to Earth.
   - **Boss:** Ravun (Alien Lord).
   - **Mechanic:** Bullet-hell + Complex Platforming.
